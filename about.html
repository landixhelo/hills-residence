About Hill's Residence

Located in Sarandë, Hill's Residence provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi. Some units at the property feature a terrace with a city view.

Popular points of interest near the hotel include Sarande Main Beach, Saranda City Beach and La Petite Beach. The nearest airport is Ioannina Airport, 60 miles from Hill's Residence.
